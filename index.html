Let's play some chess?
Register here.

The idea is to contribute to an NGO depending on the results of an online chess game. This is how it will work - Register on the website and I will reach you out for a game of chess. For every match I lose, I shall donate ₹50 on behalf of the winner. For every match I win, the opponent shall donate ₹50. Each participant shall be kept in loop about the donation payment to the NGO.
